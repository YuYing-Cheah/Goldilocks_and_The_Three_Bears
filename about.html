Goldilocks and
The Three Bears



A curious little girl named Goldilocks wanders into the forest and discovers a house belonging to three bears: Papa Bear, Mama Bear, and Baby Bear. While they are away, she enters the house uninvited and tries their porridge, chairs, and beds. She finds Papa Bear's and Mama Bear's belongings too extreme for her, but Baby Bear's are "just right." Eventually, she falls asleep in Baby Bear's bed. When the bears return and find evidence of her intrusion, they are shocked. Goldilocks wakes up, sees the bears... What’s she’s going to do after? Her fate is by your hands with one click.

Goldilocks

A curious, adventurous little girl who stumbles upon the bears' house in the forest. Seeking the "just right" fit, showing her impulsive and slightly mischievous nature.

Baby Bear

The youngest and smallest of the family. His porridge, chair, and bed are "just right" for Goldilocks, which leads to the climax when the bears discover her.

Mama Bear

The nurturing and caring figure. Her belongings are medium-sized but still not quite right for Goldilocks.

Papa Bear

The large, strong figure of the bear family. His belongings are always the largest and not suitable for Goldilocks.
